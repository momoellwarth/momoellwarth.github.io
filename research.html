Research

I study how solar surface structures and magnetic activity affect spectral line profiles and radial velocity measurements.

My work uses high-resolution solar spectroscopy to investigate the Sun both as a resolved stellar surface and as a Sun-as-a-star reference for stellar activity studies.

Research interests

  • Sun-as-a-star spectroscopy
  • Line bisectors and convective line shifts
  • Solar magnetic activity and radial velocities
  • High-resolution spectral atlases
  • Connecting solar and stellar activity diagnostics
